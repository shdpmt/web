DPP: Chapter 1 - Kinematics

1. What is the SI unit of velocity?

The SI unit of velocity is "meter per second" because velocity is displacement divided by time.

2. What is the slope of a velocity-time graph?

The slope of a velocity-time graph represents acceleration, as acceleration is the rate of change of velocity.
